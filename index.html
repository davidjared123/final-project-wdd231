Site Plan: Music Academy Website

Site Name

Music Academy Website – This name clearly identifies the purpose of the site: an academy focused on teaching music through lessons in piano, guitar, and singing.

Optional domain: musicacademy.org

Site Purpose

The website will serve as an informational hub for the Music Academy. It will introduce the academy, showcase the teachers, and provide a contact form so that prospective students can reach out for more information. The goal is to create a professional and engaging online presence for the academy.

Scenarios

  • “Who are the teachers at the academy, and what instruments do they specialize in?”
  • “How can I contact the academy to ask about lesson schedules and pricing?”

Color Scheme

The chosen colors reflect a modern and professional look while also being warm and inviting for music students:

  • #007BFF (Primary Blue): Used for headings, links, and important accents.
  • #6C757D (Softer Blue): Used for subheadings and secondary text.
  • #F8F9FA (Light Background): Used for the page background to ensure a clean and readable layout.

Typography

Selected fonts are:

  • Merriweather (serif): Used for headings (H1, H2) to give a classic, formal look.
  • Roboto (sans-serif): Used for body text for readability and modern style.
